Update an existed employee

To do this, you choose the employee that you want to update information by clicking on the employee name. Then click on button Update at the bottom of the frame. If you do not select one employee before editing, an information message will be displayed to remind you.

After that, you update the information that you want to change. For the unchanged information, you do NOT need to type again. Click the button Save to make the change effective or click Cancel to undo the change.
