Add a new contract

An employee can have many contracts during working in the restaurant, but just only one contract is effective at one time. The column "Had contract?" in the Employee List shows if one employee has some contract or not. To add a new contract for an employee, you do as follows:

  • Click the desired employee in the Employee List
  • Click the New Contract button on the window
  • Choose the position title of the employee
  • Input the date when the contract is effective
  • Choose the duration of the contract
  • Click the button Save
