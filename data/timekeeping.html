Do the timekeeping

You do the following steps:

  • Choose the employee in the Employee List that you want to record the time. You can also search some employee by inputting the name (first name, middle name, last name, or both of them). Then you click the button Search.
  • Select month and year to do the timekeeping.
  • Check the check-box in the column Working if that employee is present at the restaurant.
  • Click button Save after you finish doing the timekeeping. Otherwise, the timekeeping record is erased afterward.

Note: You can do the timekeeping if the following conditions are satisfied:

  1. The desired employee has a contract.
  2. The selected month and year belong to the duration of one contract. It means that the selected time is after the start date and before the end date of the contract.
